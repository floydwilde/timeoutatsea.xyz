Alexandra Lulu Orange touched the lives of many people, there is this thread on the tribe.net dadaist group. A person who knew her has posted some pictures of her on his website.

We are interested in hearing other peoples memories of Lulu, and if you have any words, pictures, sounds Lulu posted online at one time or another, please consider contributing them here. We have a few things, and know there is a lot more out there. If there is a memory or an ancedote about her, and you would like to write about it here, we would love to see it.

Lulu had an interesting life, online and off. This site aims to preserve a memory of her online life, and while we are interested in more personal details about who she really was, this is not the focus of the site. She was a student of archeology, and we would like to preserve some digital artifacts about her here.

Sorry the site is a little disorganized right now. It's maintained by a few who knew her online, and is completely unauthorized.
