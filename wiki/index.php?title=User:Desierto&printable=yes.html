(aka Floyd Wilde aka John B)

A short memory of Lulu, that I wrote on a test wiki install I made on my home computer, which lead to the realization of this site.

I remember staying up way past my bedtime, to talk to Lulu. Now I'm staying up late working on this site.

One time Lulu asked me for advice about if she should take steroids, which a doctor or someone had suggested to her. I replied to her that only if they give you special sexual powers. I was worried she would think I was not taking the situation seriously and if I should have said that, but she seemed very amused, and I was glad I was able to make her laugh.

A poem I wrote to Lulu upon her showing me a picture of herself, with long hair combed over her face, revealing one wide open, yet tired eye. In many of her pictures only one eye is visible. Well at least two I can recall, that one over there in the upper left hand corner for instance. We often thought of her as a muse, so the allusion to the odyssey seemed appropriate.

overcome 

 sleep encrusted
 cyclopic siren
 across the sea
 
 scent of a voice
 shoulder hair
 lips shadow
 
 wisdom
 love

(the time stamp says 2003-05-26 10:34:00)
